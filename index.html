About me

I am a second year PhD student at University of Warwick, supervised by Marcin Jurdzinski. Before coming to Warwick, I was at Chennai Mathematical Institute from 2018 to 2021 for my undergraduate in Mathematics and Computer Science.

Research Interests

Broadly, I am interested in Automata and Games that are relevant to Logic. More recently, I have been looking at Parity Tree Automata, and the notion of History Determinism in the setting of Automata over Infinite words, and Pushdown Automata.

Teaching

  • I am currently a Teaching Assistant in Formal Languages (CS259) and Algorithms(CS260).
  • I was a Teaching Assistant at Chennai Mathematical Institute for the following courses : Discrete Mathematics, Algebra III, Theory of Computation.
  • I was also a lecturer and a resource person at Indian Maths Olympiad Training Camp (IMOTC) in the years 2019,2020 and 2021.
